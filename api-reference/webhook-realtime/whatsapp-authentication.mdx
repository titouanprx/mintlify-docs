## Authentication Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create WhatsApp Account">
    QR code generation starts immediately after account creation
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive QR Code">
    Your `qr_webhook_url` receives the QR code image automatically
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    User scans the QR code with their WhatsApp mobile app (20 sec window)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authentication Complete">
    Account becomes authenticated and monitoring begins
  </Step>
</Steps>


## QR Code Lifecycle

- QR codes are generated immediately after account creation
- Each QR code expires after 20 sec
- New QR codes are automatically generated and sent to your webhook
- Display the latest QR code received
